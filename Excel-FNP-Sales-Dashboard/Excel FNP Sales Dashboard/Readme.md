# 🎁 FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Microsoft Excel dashboard** built using the **Ferns and Petals (FNP) Sales Dataset** to analyze sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, product trends, and delivery efficiency across various gifting occasions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard. The dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), explore sales trends, and make data-driven business decisions using filters and visualizations.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Ferns and Petals (FNP) wanted to analyze its sales data to answer important business questions such as:

- What is the total revenue generated?
- How much does an average customer spend?
- What is the average delivery time?
- Which occasions generate the highest revenue?
- Which product categories and products perform the best?
- How do monthly sales vary throughout the year?
- Which cities contribute the highest number of orders?
- How are orders distributed across the days of the week?

This dashboard consolidates all these insights into a single interactive report.

Interactive slicers allow users to filter the dashboard based on different occasions for deeper analysis.

---

## 📌 Key Findings

- Generated a total revenue of **₹35.2 Lakhs** from **1,000 customer orders**.
- Customers spent an average of **₹3,520.98** per order.
- The average delivery time was approximately **5.5 days**.
- Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan were among the highest revenue-generating occasions.
- Soft Toys emerged as one of the top-performing product categories.
- Monthly sales showed seasonal variations, helping identify peak business periods.
- A few cities accounted for a significant share of total orders, highlighting important target markets.
